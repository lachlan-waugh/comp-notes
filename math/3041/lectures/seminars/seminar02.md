## Battle of Trafalgar

> $B_n$: number of British ships at stage $n$
> $F_n$: number of French-Spanish ships at stage $n$

Number of ships remaining after stage $n$:
> $B_{n+1}=B_n-0.05F_n$
> $F_{n+1}=F_n-0.05B_n$
